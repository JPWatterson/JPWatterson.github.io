About Inside/50

What is Inside/50?

The Inside/50 responsive web app is designed to help young footballers find, enter & excel in Australian Rules football pathways to achieve their goal of reaching the elite level of the sport.

Our aim is to give players, coaches & parents alike the foundations to nurture elite footballing talent all around Australia by providing information on player pathways & giving users the ability to track key statistical areas.

How does Inside/50 work?

Our users can utilise our Stat Counter to keep track of their gameday statistics in real-time and allow live feedback to be given to the players during their games so that they can improve on their deficient areas.

User can also see a list of the current captains of each AFL team as well as the amount of games they have played in an easy to digest format as well as find their local talent pathways available in their state by either searching for the name in the filter section or by clicking their respective state on the interactive image of Australia.
